Responsive Typography

In this exercise, you will adjust the font size of headers and paragraphs to enhance readability on larger screens.

Instructions:

  • Begin with styles suitable for small screens and progressively increase font sizes as the screen width expands. The initial paragraph font size should be 1rem, then 1.25rem, then finally 1.5 rem.

  • Utilize media queries for a minimum width at breakpoints of 600px and 900px.
